My Road

I have been where you are

I opened my salon at 21 with no education, no mentor, and no map. It nearly cost me every soft thing in me.

Fifteen years in the beauty industry. I founded a salon in London, scaled it to a team of eight, and did everything the ambitious woman is supposed to do — first in, last out, always available, running on caffeine and the fear of dropping a single plate.

From the outside it was a success story. From the inside, I was The Machine, The Ghost, and The Firefighter on rotation. Then I burnt out completely — in the way that only happens when you have been holding everything together alone for years.

I invested £85,000 into a supplement brand and lost most of it. I moved to Sydney and rebuilt deliberately. What I teach now, I learned by living it — not by studying it.

The Power Reset is the method I built doing it — the one I wish someone had handed me at the salon, at midnight, doing payroll for the third time that week.

I'm not ahead of you. I'm just further down the same road.
